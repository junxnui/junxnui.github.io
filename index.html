I am currently a PhD student in the Department of Computer Science, University of Hong Kong, under the supervision of Dr. Li-Yi Wei. My work focuses on a hybrid research of Graphics and Human Computer Interaction (HCI). In particular, I am interested in analyzing the repetitions in human-centered activities, such as painting and writing, and providing online “intelligent” suggestions, via a natural interface, to reduce manual labor while improving quality and performance. I am also interested in machine/deep learning for text, image and video analysis and generation, VR/AR for content creation, and user experience design.


I got my Bachelor's Degree in Electronic Engineering and Information Science in University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), where my research was about Super-resolution of single image.
