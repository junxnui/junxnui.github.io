Co-worker: Chao Tian, Xiangyu Yan
Time: 2011.9 This is a project of CUMCM competition, our main task was to predict the distribution of the heavy metal elements across the city according to data of different areas such as the residential area and the indstrial area. The problem of the contest is to locate the pollution source of all the heavy metals. We use matlab to simulation the distribution of the heavy metals in the city, as to locate the pollution source. We use the Sparse Representation Theory and the Gauss-Spread Model to simulate the spread and by combing the two method we got good results. [more]
